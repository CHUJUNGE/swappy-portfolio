About the Swappy Web Prototype

The Swappy web prototype is a fully functional demonstration of our sustainable campus exchange platform. This interactive prototype showcases the complete user experience of the Swappy platform, allowing users to:

Home Page

Explore the main features, weekly themes, and featured items available on the platform.

Browse Items

View and filter through available items for exchange on the platform.

Upload Item

Experience the item submission process with our user-friendly upload form.

Rules & Contact

Access platform guidelines and find ways to get in touch with the Swappy team.

The prototype demonstrates our token-based exchange system, where users can upload items they no longer need, receive tokens upon approval, and then use those tokens to acquire items from other users. This sustainable approach promotes reuse and reduces waste within the university community.

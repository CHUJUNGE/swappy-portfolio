About Swappy

Sustainable Exchange for University Community

Swappy is a web-based platform designed to promote sustainability within the University of Melbourne community by facilitating the exchange of items between students and staff.

The platform operates on a token-based system, where users can upload items they no longer need, receive tokens upon approval, and then use those tokens to acquire items from other users. This creates a circular economy within the university, reducing waste and promoting reuse.

With weekly themed exchanges and a user-friendly interface, Swappy makes sustainable choices easy and engaging for the entire university community.

Key Features

Token-Based Exchange

Users receive tokens for items they contribute and can redeem them for other items, creating a fair and balanced exchange system.

Weekly Themes

Each week features a different category of items, encouraging diverse contributions and keeping the platform fresh and engaging.

Advanced Filtering

Users can easily find items through advanced search and filtering options, including categories, conditions, and keywords.

Accessibility Features

Built with accessibility in mind, including screen reader compatibility and an accessibility mode toggle.

Quality Control

All items are reviewed by our team to ensure they meet quality standards before being listed on the platform.

Community Building

Fosters a sense of community among university members through shared sustainability goals.
